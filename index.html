Multidisciplinary Marketing & Growth Advisor, Entrepreneur, and Nomad in Sunny L.A

Building, Scaling, and Guiding Culture Shaping Companies

Mischa Armada is a multidisciplinary marketing leader helping clients implement large-scale growth programs for a variety of consumer-facing businesses — from being part of the original team that launched Tinder to leading marketing at high-growth SaaS companies.

With a particular focus on multichannel go-to-market strategy, digital strategy, and product marketing, her expertise with a wide range of brands in a constantly evolving digital landscape allows her to help clients identify the most valuable areas to generate growth, discover new business opportunities, and optimize customer experiences that lead to greater operational efficiencies and stronger revenue growth.

A happy blend of marketer and technologist, Mischa combines her passion for creative storytelling, deep understanding of consumer behavior, and ability to translate data into impactful marketing strategies to lead cross-functional teams and execute tactics that incite growth across all consumer touch-points — Increasing awareness, revenue, market share, and mind share.
